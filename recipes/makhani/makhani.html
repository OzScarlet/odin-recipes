Chicken Makhani - Indian Butter Chicken

Description

This butter chicken recipe, or chicken makhani, is one of my favorite Indian dishes. It features a full-flavored sauce that complements the chicken well. It can be made as mild or spicy as you wish by adjusting the cayenne. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

Ingredients

  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garam Masala
  • Chili Powder
  • Ground Cumin
  • Bay Leaf
  • Tomato Puree
  • Half-and-half
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Boneless, skinless Chicken
  • Cayenne Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oil, saute shallot and onion.
  2. Add others except chicken.
  3. Heat oil, cook chicken in separate pan.
  4. Reduce heat, season with garam masala and cayenne.
  5. Mix into sauce, cook until thickened.
