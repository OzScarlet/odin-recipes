Double Layer Pumpkin Cheesecake

Description

This pumpkin cheesecake is a great alternative to traditional cheesecake â€” especially for pumpkin pie fans! A thick, creamy cheesecake base topped with a layer of spiced pumpkin cheesecake filling sits on a graham cracker crust in this easy layered holiday dessert. Two flavors of cheesecake in every bite! Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • White Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs
  • Graham Cracker Crust
  • Pumpkin Puree
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Ground Cloves
  • Ground Nutmeg

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Make cheesecake layer.
  3. Spread batter in the graham cracker crust.
  4. Make pumpkin layer.
  5. Spread on top of plain cheesecake batter in the crust.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven.
  7. Let rest, refrigerate before serving.
